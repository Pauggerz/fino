Redesign Notes

What changed and why

① NET WORTH HERO CARD NEW
Dark green card showing total net worth, assets vs liabilities, and account count. Gives the screen a strong anchor instead of jumping straight into the list. Reuses the existing hero-bg color token.
② BALANCE DOT INDICATORS NEW
A 6px colored dot (green = positive, red = negative) before each balance. Lets users scan account health at a glance without reading every number. Balance amounts also colored red when negative.
③ TOOLS → 2×2 GRID REDESIGNED
Tools changed from a plain vertical list to a 2-column tile grid. Each tile has icon + name + short description. More visual, easier to navigate, no chevrons needed since tiles are obviously tappable.
④ BILL CARD — AMOUNT ON ROW
Amount moved into the meta line ("Due today · ₱100,000") instead of floating on the right — reduces visual clutter on narrow cards. Left accent bar added for urgency.
⑤ CONDENSED BALANCES
Balances show without decimal places (₱69,420 vs ₱69,420.00). Reduces visual noise in the list. Full precision still shown on the Account Detail screen.
