v2 changes vs v1

  1. Single scroll grouped into 4 numbered "answer-groups" (Where you stand · Where it went · When you spend · The flow).
  2. Fino Intelligence threaded through: 1 lavender headline at the top + 1 short chip above each group.
  3. Trajectory is the headline; Daily Spend chart lives in a collapsible "View daily breakdown" drill underneath.
  4. Composition group brings back the Donut, By Account horizontal strip, and a Top Spending card with a Merchants ↔ Transactions toggle (top 5 merchants vs top 5 single transactions).
  5. DOW Patterns chart kept as its own "When you spend" group with avg weekday/weekend/peak chips.
  6. Numbers still cross-consistent: Income ₱40,250 − Expense ₱26,000 = +₱14,250 = Sankey Savings node. Donut totals = ₱26,000 = By-Account totals = trajectory endpoint. Subscriptions ₱1,847 sit inside Bills ₱5,400.
  7. Dropped: expense/income view-type toggle, the 3-tab Spend/Patterns/Categories nav, the WaveFill category tiles, the standalone budget-alert card.
  8. Quick scroll: a sticky 4-tab nav (01 Stand · 02 Where · 03 When · 04 Flow) pins to the top once you scroll past the Fino headline. Tap a tab to smooth-jump; the active tab tracks the current section as you scroll.
